Know Your NSTSE

NSTSE is a diagnostic test which helps students improve their overall learning ability and educational performance.

Unlike other tests which may only find out how much a student knows or has memorised, NSTSE measures how well a student has understood the concepts and provides a detailed feedback on the same, to help him/her improve.

Few Key Features of Exam

The most promising and confidence building exam

Very much structured, in depth and unique in nature compared to other exams

Students have the liberty to opt for their subjects from a widely selected list

Certificate carries enormous acknowledgement and prestige all over the world

Exam conducted in accordance with the recommendations of the NEP 1986

It is only through the medium of English


Candidates to enter and sit for 6 subjects and Socially Useful Productive Work

The examination pre-supposes a school course of ten years duration

Private candidates are not permitted to appear for the examination

Support

Performance scorecard helps an individual to evaluate themselves through Facilited self discovery (FSD), Notes Boards Faculty (NBF) and Concept maps and content (CMC). FSD is important as it helps the student to assess their performance, identify their strong and weak chapters . Its features are that the student gets subject-wise chapter test, multiple chapter test. NBF is beneficial as it allows student to attend classes at their own space , that is , they can avail online classes which helps them to understand the concept better and watch the videos whenever they have doubts. In NBF, live classes are conducted by expert faculties , where students get to take full course/crash course/top-up class. Last but not the least , aspect of Crestest is CMC. CMC is a pictorial representation of the chapter which is redirected to content so that the students can read specific topics in details.
